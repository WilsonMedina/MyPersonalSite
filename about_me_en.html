I am Wilson Medina Cárdenas, Venezuelan by birth, resident of the world and for a little over 7 years I have lived in the beautiful city of Santiago de Chile.

Much of my time is spent studying and teaching technology, statistics, and mathematics. oh! and not least, to share with my dogs Nesky and kuky. I love them!

But it has not always been like this, my life in the last decade has gone through different stages that have helped me grow as a person and get me to where I am now.

It has been like a roller coaster with exciting ups and terrifying downs, the love for my family in Venezuela, and knowing that I am part of their economic and emotional sustenance, is what in these terrifying descents has not made me lose consciousness to the point of fainting.

As a child my greatest passion was soccer, I lived for and to play it, discipline helped me to reach those training sessions and games despite living almost 2 hours away, always parallel to my studies, but with the faithful conviction that I would play at a professional level. That until the age of 17, when I had a severe fall while playing a match, which resulted in an injury that I did not I recovered but after 5 years; It was at that moment when I hit the harsh reality and my dreams collapsed

At the age of 22, I entered the university to study administration specializing in Marketing, when I was about to complete the cycle of professional subjects to be able to graduate, I had to be part of Venezuelan migrants.

Arriving in another country to work and being able to fulfill my commitments as a resident of this one, I force myself for a long time to not being able to study; I could only work very hard 💪 and without rest, Only that formula I take with my wife to have savings that I later invested in an SME of transport and then transformed at a food distributor.

It has been this stage that introduced me to many valuable people in my life, people that i taught a lot and what I value most of those teachings is having discovered the beautiful and infinite world of technology 💻. That world that does not stop, that teaches you to something new every day, that world that keeps you in constant harmony, peace and growth, that world for which today I have the same emotions, commitments and discipline of that Wilson who played soccer as a child.

At present I resumed my distance studies at the same university, I am already studying the Last year of administration, but specialized in Computer Science. In parallel, as self taught i have learned a lot about various topics and it has also been an alternative that, despite not being the traditional has taught me my greatest skills.

It is at this point in my life that Steve Jobs's speech where he leaves us a valuable lesson comes to mind the most:

“You can't connect the dots looking forward, you can only do it looking back”

Just looking back is what helps me understand why I couldn't play professional soccer, because I couldn't finish my university degree at that time, because I had to be a migrant, because I met such valuable people. It is uniting all these points that gives me an answer and a reason for life in the present.

“Genius is made with 1% talent and 99% work”

Albert Einstein
